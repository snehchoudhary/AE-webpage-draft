Built for the Complete Health of Your Pipeline

T-PIMS™ is a comprehensive integrity management framework, designed using industry-leading practices and years of operational expertise. Developed by AE, it systematically segments your pipeline network—factoring in every technical detail that could impact performance—Identifying Important steps to ensure long-term reliability, reduced risk, and operational excellence.

Why T-PIMS™?

In today’s environment, pipeline integrity isn’t optional—it’s critical. As demand for energy continues to rise and maintenance costs soar, managing pipeline health has never been more complex or more essential. Failures can put people, the environment, and assets at risk. That’s why AE’s T-PIMS™ brings together data, insight, and action in one unified system. Backed by two decades of experience, 95+ published studies on integrity, and hundreds of successful projects, T-PIMS™ is not just a tool—it’s the most advanced integrity partner you can have.

From high-level risk strategy to inch-by-inch field insight, T-PIMS™ is your one-stop solution.
